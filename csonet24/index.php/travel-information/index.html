Travel Information

Dear all,

We are excited to welcome you to CSoNet 2024, the international conference, hosted at Thammasat University. Below are important travel instructions to help you reach the conference venue smoothly.

Conference Venue

The conference will be held at: Institute of East Asian Studies , Thammasat University (Rangsit Campus), Pathum Thani, Thailand.

Important Note: Ensure you travel to Rangsit Campus, not the Prachan Campus in Bangkok, to avoid confusion.

Transportation Recommendations

Airport Taxi
- Upon arrival at BKK Suvarnabhumi Airport or DMK Don Mueang Airport, take an airport taxi.
- Inform the dirver you are going to "Thammasat Rangist" -- this destination is widely recognized.
- Provide the following location for accuracy Institute of East Asian Studies - Google Maps
- The journey will take approximately 40 minutes. We recommend requesting the driver to take the expressway (Tollway) for faster travel.
Grab App
- For a more convenient and flexible travel option, download the Grab app (available on iOS and Android).
- Grab allows you to book taxis or private cars directly through your phone.

Additional Assistance

If you need further support or have any questions, please join the CSoNet Support WhatsApp Group for real-time assistance:CSoNet Support WhatsApp Group

We hope you have a pleasant journey and look forward to seeing you at CSoNet 2024!

Best Regards,

CSoNet 2024 Organizing Commitee
