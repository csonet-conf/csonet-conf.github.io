Registration for CSoNet 2021 allows full access to the conference and all sessions. At least one author of each accepted paper must register at the regular full rate (a student registration cannot<\/em> cover the publication of a paper). An author with multiple accepted papers must pay the full registration fee for each accepted paper.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

When submitting the final version of your paper, include a copy of the registration confirmation (or receipt)<\/strong> in the submission bundle. The final submission with receipt must be received by September 24th<\/strong> to guarantee inclusion in the proceedings. Extra page fee will be 100 USD per page for up to 2 extra pages.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Registration fees<\/h4>\n\n\n\n
  • Full Author Registration (by Sep. 24th<\/strong>; full\/short paper and extended abstract): $50<\/li>
  • Early Full Registration without Paper (by Oct. 15th): $50<\/li>
  • Late Full Registration without Paper (after Oct. 15th): $75<\/li>
  • Early Student Registration (by Oct. 15th): $20<\/li>
  • Late Student Registration (after Oct. 15th): $30<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    Refund policy:<\/strong> registrations can be canceled by Nov. 12th with a cancellation fee ($20 for Full non-author registrations and $10 for Student registrations). Full author registrations are non-refundable.<\/em><\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
