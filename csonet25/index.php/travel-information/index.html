Travel Information & Transportation Recommendations

Most international visitors to Vietnam are required to obtain a visa prior to arrival, unless your country is on Vietnam’s visa exemption list (please check the latest list on the official website of the Vietnam Immigration Department or with your local Vietnamese Embassy/Consulate).

We highly recommend applying for a Vietnamese e-visa, which is available online for citizens of over 80 countries (https://evisa.gov.vn/)

Entry & Arrival at Danang Airport

No COVID‑19 entry requirements as of mid‑2022: no vaccine proof, test, insurance, or health declaration form.

Passengers simply go through immigration with valid passport and visa/e‑Visa.

Please ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months beyond your planned date of entry into Vietnam and has at least two blank pages for visa stamps.

Getting around Da Nang

Taxi/Grab: We recommend using reliable taxi companies such as Mai Linh, Vinasun, or using ride-hailing apps like Grab, Xanh SM for a safe and convenient transfer.

Car Rental with Driver: Recommended for groups or those needing flexible transportation for a day, including day trips to destinations such as Hue Citadel, Hoi An Ancient Town, or Ba Na Hills.

Motorbike Rental: Available for experienced riders, but please note traffic regulations and safety requirements.

Public Transportation: City buses operate on limited routes; schedules may not be convenient for visitors.
