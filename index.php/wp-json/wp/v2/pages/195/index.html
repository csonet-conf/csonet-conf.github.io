Registration for CSoNet 2021 allows full access to the conference and all sessions. At least one author of each accepted paper must register at the regular full rate (a student registration cannot cover the publication of a paper). An author with multiple accepted papers must pay the full registration fee for each accepted paper.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

When submitting the final version of your paper, include a copy of the\u00a0registration confirmation (or receipt)<\/strong> in the submission bundle. Extra page fee will be 100 USD per page for up to 2 extra pages.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
